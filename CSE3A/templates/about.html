About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a smart platform designed to connect students, faculty, and administration in one place. It helps in sharing information, managing events, and improving communication across the campus.

🎯 Our Mission

To simplify campus communication and provide a digital platform where students can easily access information, share knowledge, and stay updated.

🚀 Our Vision

To create a connected and smart campus environment where technology enhances learning, collaboration, and overall student experience.
